                                 INVENTORY MANAGEMENT 
The construction industry plays a vital role in national development, but it still 
faces major challenges due to the lack of proper management and automation. Traditionally, 
construction sites depend on manual records and spreadsheets to track materials, attendance, 
and financial transactions, which often leads to errors, confusion, and miscommunication. 
These outdated methods make it difficult to maintain accurate data, monitor expenses, and 
ensure accountability among site workers. Material shortages, delayed procurements, and 
unrecorded transactions further slow down the overall progress of projects. Existing digital 
tools, though available, are often costly, complex, or not designed for small and medium-scale 
construction projects. As a result, many local contractors and site engineers still rely on manual 
processes, leading to inefficiency and poor coordination. There is a strong need for a cost
effective, user-friendly system that provides centralized control over materials, labour, and site 
activities while ensuring transparency and accuracy in operations. 


In this project, we are going to develop a role-based Construction Inventory 
Management System that streamlines material tracking, labor attendance, financial 
transactions, and material request operations. The system is divided into four core modules: 
Material Management, Attendance System, Transactions, and Request Module. Role-based 
login ensures data privacy and controlled access, where the admin manages all modules, the 
site engineer can raise material requests, and the inventory manager can view and process these 
requests. The system replaces traditional manual tracking with an automated digital platform 
that minimizes errors and improves efficiency. It helps in monitoring stock levels, maintaining 
labor data, and tracking expenditures in real time. By integrating all key operations, the project 
ensures better communication between different site roles and avoids unnecessary delays. The 
solution enhances transparency, improves decision-making, and ensures cost control through 
proper record maintenance. Overall, this project provides a simple yet effective approach to 
digitalizing construction site management, helping organizations achieve higher productivity, 
accountability, and streamlined workflow.
